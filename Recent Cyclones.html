RECENT CYCLONES IN  I N D I A

YAAS

The cyclones are classified as severe with MSW of 48-63 knots, very severe with MSW of 64-89 knots, extremely severe with MSW of 90-119 knots,
and super cyclonic storms with MSW of 120 knots.

ASANI

Severe Cyclonic Storm Asani was a strong tropical cyclone that made landfall in India in May 2022. The third depression and deep depression,
and the first named storm of the 2022 North Indian Ocean cyclone season.
